## 2. Free preview contents

The buyer enters: district → tehsil → village → search type (Khata or Plot) → identifier → optional seller name → email.

The free preview returns:

- **Plot found:** confirmation that the plot exists in Bhulekh for the given district/tehsil/village/identifier
- **Owner name (masked):** first name + last initial only (e.g. *"Lipika S."*)
- **Kisam:** in plain English (e.g. *"Agricultural — paddy"* not just the Odia term)
- **Map pin:** plot location on a generic map (no satellite, no boundary in free preview)
- **One-line summary:** *"Plot found. 12 decimals. Agricultural kisam. 1 listed owner."* (or whatever fits)
- **Conversion CTA:** *"Get the full report for ₹999 — see what you might lose after paying, what you can build, and what it's worth."*

The free preview is the buyer's trust test. If it correctly identifies their plot, they believe the rest.

## 3. Standard tier contents (the ₹999 report)

### 3.1 Six sections, in this order

| # | Section | What it answers |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **The plot** | "Where is this plot, and what does it look like?" |
| 2 | **The owner** | "Does the seller actually own this?" |
| 3 | **What you might lose after paying** | "Can I lose money after I pay?" |
| 4 | **What you can build here** | "Can I build the house/structure I want?" |
| 5 | **What it's worth** | "Am I overpaying?" |
| 6 | **What to do before you pay** | "What's my next step?" |

### 3.2 Three depths, one report

Same report, layered for different readers:

- **Top layer — WhatsApp-shareable summary (1 screen).** Plot address + owner name + area + top 3 things to know (green/amber/red) + total quantified exposure + report-generated timestamp + verification link. Footer: *"Generated by ClearDeed — get one for your property."*
- **Middle layer — Buyer's read (5–7 screens).** Each of the six sections, in plain English, with the most important facts visible and details collapsible.
- **Bottom layer — Lawyer's drill-down.** Source-by-source raw data, parser confidence, provenance links to Bhulekh / IGR / eCourts / RCCMS / CERSAI pages.

### 3.3 Cross-cutting rules

- **Every fact links to its source.** No claim without provenance.
- **Positive signals first, watch-outs second** within each section. Reports default to neutral; they do not alarm unnecessarily.
- **Market data is framed as floor / directional / ceiling.** No composite scores. Never a number ClearDeed owns legally.
- **District-specific disclaimer block** on every report (CRZ for Puri, flood for Cuttack, etc.).
- **Founder review** of every LLM-translated report before email send. Launch-blocking through first 500 reports.

### 3.4 Out of scope at launch

- **Social proof** (testimonials, live report counter, anonymized report excerpt) — `COMMERCIAL_TRACK.md` §2. Ships after first real buyers exist.
- **Buyer interviews and customer development** — `COMMERCIAL_TRACK.md` §1. Begins after PI 3.
- **Guaranteed tier, District Pass, self-serve B2B API** — deliberately deferred. See `DECISIONS.md`.

### 4.3 What you might lose after paying (the financial exposure section)

| Data | Source | Sprint live |
|---|---|---|
| Encumbrance Certificate (mortgage entries) | IGR Odisha — automated scraping | S3 |
| Court attachments / lis pendens | eCourts India + manual check | S3 |
| Pending revenue cases | RCCMS Odisha | S3 |
| Active mortgage check | CERSAI | S3 |
| BMC property tax outstanding | BMC portal | S11 (PI 3) |

**Quantified exposure formulas:**

| Risk | Exposure formula | Display |
|---|---|---|
| Active mortgage (CERSAI hit) | Mortgage amount or estimated 60–80% of property value | *"Lender may recover up to ₹X from the property if loan is not discharged before sale."* |
| Court attachment | Full purchase value | *"If attachment is upheld, full purchase consideration of ₹X is at risk."* |
| Pending revenue case | 30% of property value + ₹2–5L litigation cost over 3–7 years | *"Estimated exposure: ₹X + litigation cost."* |
| Unauthorized construction on agri kisam | Demolition + conversion + 2–3 year delay | *"Demolition risk. Conversion fee for N decimals ≈ ₹X."* |
| PoA-based sale (Suraj Lamp risk) | Full property value | *"PoA-based conveyances are not valid title transfers per Supreme Court. Full ₹X at risk."* |
| Lease land sold as freehold (Patia pattern) | Full property value | *"If land is resumed by government, full ₹X is at risk."* |
| Outstanding property tax | Tax outstanding | *"Outstanding ₹X. Becomes buyer's liability unless cleared at registration."* |

**Headline:** *"Verified clear exposure: ₹0 on [list]. Quantified at-risk exposure: ₹X across [list]. Unquantified items requiring manual verification: [list]."*

### 4.5 What it's worth

| Data | Source | Sprint live | Framing |
|---|---|---|---|
| Circle rate | IGR Odisha circle rate tables | S4 | **Floor** — *"Government floor. Sale will not register below this."* |
| Recent IGR-recorded sales in village (last 24 months) | IGR Odisha sale records | S4 | **Directional** — *"Registered values reflect 50–80% of market price due to stamp duty optimization. Treat as floor + signal, not target."* |
| Current broker listings in the locality | MagicBricks / 99acres / OLX scrape | S4 | **Ceiling** — *"Aspirational asking prices. Treat as upper bound."* |

No composite score. No "fair value estimate" ClearDeed owns. The three signals together let the buyer triangulate.

## 5. Fraud detection rules (cross-referenced with FRAUD_PATTERNS.md)

These are detection rules, not predictions. A flagged report tells the buyer to look harder; it does not call the seller a fraud.

| Pattern | Detection rule | Sprint live |
|---|---|---|
| **Patia industrial-lease scam** | If Satwa = Sthitiban/Raiyati BUT IGR shows prior lease deed (RLD prefix) → CRITICAL | S3 |
| **Surya Nirman multi-investor fraud** | Plot/khata ∩ EOW-attached property list → CRITICAL; owner ∩ EOW arrest list → HIGH | S11 |
| **Malipada impersonation** | Seller's PAN/Aadhaar-linked address materially distant from plot AND no local PoA → HIGH | S3 (manual) → S11 (auto) |
| **Industrial-zone-sold-as-residential** | Plot GPS ∩ BDA Industrial Zone layer → CRITICAL | S4 |
| **Sub-plot without BDA layout approval** | Plot number ~ regex `/[0-9]+/[0-9A-Z]+` OR `/D\/[0-9]+/` AND no BDA layout approval → HIGH | S4 (regex) → S11 (approval registry) |

## 6. Delivery

- **Token-scoped persistent URL** for the web report. Sharable.
- **Mobile-responsive web view.** Most buyers open on phone first.
- **Server-rendered PDF** from the same HTML as the web report. Attached to delivery email.
- **Email delivery** on payment confirmation (Razorpay webhook → Resend send).
- **SMS as link-only fallback.** No SMS-delivered report content.
- **Print-optimized CSS** for buyers walking the PDF to a lawyer or bank.
- **Shareable read-only mode** strips the financial exposure calculator (so a family member doesn't accidentally trigger a panic from a calculator they can't interpret).
- **60-day report validity** with pay-to-refresh option (~₹299 for refresh).

**Not in scope:** WhatsApp delivery infrastructure. WhatsApp Business API integration. WhatsApp is a sharing channel, not a delivery channel. The report URL gets shared; the report itself does not arrive over WhatsApp.

## 7. Input form

Order matters. This is the buyer's first interaction.

```
District       (Khordha at launch; 5 districts by PI 2)
   ↓
Tehsil         (dropdown filtered by district)
   ↓
Village        (dropdown filtered by tehsil)
   ↓
Search by      (Khata | Plot)
   ↓
Identifier     (the Khata or Plot number)
   ↓
Seller name    (optional — enables name-match insight)
   ↓
Email          (final field, single Razorpay button after)
```

**No phone field required.** Email is enough. Razorpay collects phone if they want.

Single-button checkout. No multi-step flow. No upsells on the checkout page.

## 9. B2B (custom-contract initial)

First B2B customer is a Bhubaneswar law firm doing 20–50 title checks per month. Likely contract elements:

- Batch input (CSV of plot IDs, or upload of broker briefs)
- Consistent report format across the batch
- Lawyer-facing drill-down emphasized
- Optional white-label (firm name on report)
- Secure delivery (signed URLs, expiring access)
- Volume pricing — likely ₹399–₹499 per report at 20+ reports/month
- Monthly invoice, NET-15

Build for this one customer's actual contract. Productize only after a second customer wants the same shape.
