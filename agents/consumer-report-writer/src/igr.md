# Source: IGR Odisha (Encumbrance Certificate)

Last verified: 2026-05-02
Status: MANUAL INSTRUCTIONS ONLY / CONCIERGE
Owner module: agents/encumbrance-reasoner

## What it returns
Encumbrance Certificate (EC) showing registered transactions, mortgages, and liens on a specific property over a selected date range.

## Access and URL
- Primary portal: `https://www.igrodisha.gov.in/`
- EC Search: `https://igrodisha.gov.in/ecsearch`

## Authentication
Online EC search requires a registered user account (citizen login). 
Full certified copies require payment of statutory fees.

## Known edge cases
- Equitable mortgages might not always reflect in the EC if not registered; CERSAI check is recommended.
- Prior to digital migration (usually pre-2008), records must be searched manually at the Sub-Registrar Office (SRO).

## Manual verification fallback
ClearDeed V1 relies on providing buyers with specific instructions, plot details, and the deep-link to the IGR portal to conduct the search manually or via a lawyer.
