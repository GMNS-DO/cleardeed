## What it returns
Benchmark valuation (also known as circle rate or guidance value) is the minimum value at which a property can be registered in the Sub-Registrar office. It is used to calculate stamp duty and registration fees.

## Access and URL
The official portal for benchmark valuation in Odisha is typically under the Inspector General of Registration (IGR).
- Primary portal: `https://www.igrodisha.gov.in/`
- Direct link to benchmark valuation search (if available): `https://regis.odisha.gov.in/Benchmark/BMV_Search.aspx` or similar.

## Current ClearDeed status
ClearDeed V1 provides a deep-link and general guidance on how to check the circle rate. We do not programmatically extract the exact circle rate for the specific plot, as the search form often requires detailed selections of mouza, kisam (land type), and proximity to roads.

## Build assessment for concierge add-on
We add a "Market benchmark" section to the report. It provides a link for the user or their lawyer to manually verify the benchmark valuation of the plot based on its exact Kisam and location details.

## Known rates (Khordha zone guidance)
- Rural agricultural: Varies widely, often ₹20L - ₹50L per acre.
- Urban/Bhubaneswar outskirts: Can be ₹1,000 - ₹3,000+ per sqft.
- *Note: These are illustrative guidance bounds. The report explicitly tells the user to check the portal.*
